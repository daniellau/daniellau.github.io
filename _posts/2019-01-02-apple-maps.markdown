### Overview
Maps is a mapping application for Apple’s ecosystem. Features like turn-by-turn directions and proactive suggestions are built into the app.

Proactive suggestions predict the places you’re likely to go and recommends the fastest way to get there based on traffic, time of day, your location, and your schedule.
